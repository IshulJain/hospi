Technex'18 is organizing a Simulation event on the Supercomputing Infrastructure and Scientific Simulation Software on Cloud Computing platform.The platform would be provided by KOGENCE. It is an opportunity to hone your skills and know your abilities in imitating the operations of real-world processes.Webinars and online tutorials will be conducted for the benefit of participants, training them in using the platform and gearing them up for the challenge.

The event is categorized under 3 topics:

KOGENCE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE SIMULATION: It includes simulations on Tensor-Flow, Keras, Torch and Scikit-Learn libraries. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the field of computer science that attempts to construct computer systems that emulate human problem-solving behavior with the goal of understanding human intelligence.

KOGENCE META MATERIAL SIMULATIONS: It includes simulations on Density Functional Theory Codes, Hartree Fock Methods, Multi Body Quantum Chemistry Codes and Molecular Dynamics. The Material Simulation covers properties, structure, and behavior of all classes of materials which include electronic structure/properties of materials determined by ab initio and/or semi-empirical methods, atomic level properties of materials, microstructural level phenomena, continuum-level modelling pertaining to material behaviour, and modelling behaviour in service.

KOGENCE NANO PHOTONICS SIMULATION: It includes simulations on Scattering Matrix Method, Finite Difference Time Domain Methods, Rigorous Coupled Wave Analysis and Frequency Domain Methods. It covers identifying physical problems in nanophotonic that represent challenges for modeling and numerical simulation. Designing and simulation of current and next-generation optical communication systems and photonic integrated circuits at the signal propagation level are also under its domain.
