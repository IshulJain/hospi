Technex has always been known for its stellar guest lecture series - 'Think Talks' - which have been becoming increasingly attractive over the years. Technex'16 witnessed personalities hailing from varied domains coming together to share their knowledge and experience with students from around the nation.
India's leading high energy physicist - Mr Atul Gurtu, planetary geologist - Mrs Rosaly Lopes and swarm robotics researcher - Mr Gianni Di Caro presented revolutionary ideas and advancements from their respective areas of research. Mr Prabhu Chawla, one of India's finest political journalists, spoke about the development of the country's scenario over the years he's been into the profession.
